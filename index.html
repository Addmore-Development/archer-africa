About Archer Africa

Where internationalisation becomes real

Archer Africa curates programmes rooted in African innovation ecosystems, connecting participants with entrepreneurs, researchers, policymakers and cultural leaders. We partner with universities, graduate schools, and executive education programmes to design and deliver immersive study experiences.

At a time when higher education is increasingly global, many programmes remain theoretical or inaccessible to students studying remotely. Archer Africa bridges this gap.

We create learning journeys that allow students, whether on-campus, evening, executive education, or online, to experience global learning firsthand.

Why Africa

Where global ideas meet lived realities

Africa is one of the most dynamic regions shaping the future of the global economy, innovation, and leadership. Rapidly growing cities, entrepreneurial ecosystems, and cultural movements are redefining conversations about technology, sustainability, and development.

Yet despite its importance, many students studying in Europe and North America have limited opportunities to engage meaningfully with African contexts during their academic journeys.

"Africa is not simply a destination. It is a place where students encounter the complexity and creativity that define our interconnected world."

Expand Beyond Traditional Destinations

Strengthen internationalisation strategies by offering global learning opportunities outside the conventional circuit.

Engage Emerging Markets

Expose students to entrepreneurial ecosystems and diverse leadership contexts increasingly central to global conversations.

Build Meaningful Partnerships

Connect with African research institutions and universities to build active, reciprocal partnerships across the continent.

Foster Cross-Cultural Understanding

Africa challenges assumptions and reveals new ways of thinking about leadership, innovation, and global collaboration.

How We Work With Universities

A partnership built on
co-design and clarity

We know that universities need confidence before committing to international programmes. Our process is designed to be transparent, collaborative, and strategically aligned from the very first conversation.

01

Discovery Conversation

We begin with a strategic conversation to understand your institution's internationalisation goals, student profile, programme structure, and timeline.

02

Programme Co-Design

We develop a tailored programme proposal — including academic framing, itinerary, partner organisations, and learning outcomes — in close collaboration with your team.

03

Logistics and Delivery

We manage all operational elements — travel, accommodation, venue partnerships, speaker coordination, and on-the-ground facilitation — so your team can focus on the academic experience.

04

Evaluation and Growth

After each programme, we conduct structured evaluation and work with you to deepen the partnership — building toward longer-term institutional collaboration.
